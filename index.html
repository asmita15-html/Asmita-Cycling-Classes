About the Classes

I teach kids how to ride bicycles safely and confidently. Beginners are welcome and learning is fun!

What Kids Will Learn

  • Balancing the cycle
  • Pedaling correctly
  • Safe braking
  • Turning and control
  • Road safety basics

Class Details

Age: 5 – 12 years

Duration: 30 minutes per class

Location: Local park or playground

Things to Bring 🚴

  • 🪖 Helmet (Mandatory)
  • 💧 Water Bottle
  • 🎀 Hairband (for students with long hair)
  • 🦺 Safety Kit (knee pads and elbow pads)

Important: For safety reasons, students must bring a helmet to ride the bicycle.
